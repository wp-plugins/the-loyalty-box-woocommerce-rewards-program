Loyalty Box Rewards plugin enables your WooCommerce store with a Loyalty Points Program as run by http://www.theloyaltybox.com/

== Description ==

= One store or many =
Franchises or Multi-Nationals. Multiple store locations under one umbrella. Physical Stores and E-Commerce. Add a new store, existing customers instantly integrated.

= Unlimited Program Options =
Loyalty Programs | Gift Card | Referral Programs | Shared Reward | Frequency | Coalition | Pre-Paid | Bank Card | VIP Programs | You think it... Create It!

= Import Existing Program(s) =
Already running a loyalty/crm program but struggling with your current tools? No problem, just import your existing customers along with their existing card numbers and you ready to go... no interuption!

= Hundreds of Rule Options =
New Enrolment; Customer's Birthday; Spend X, Get Y; Spending Tiers; Goal reached; % Cash Back; Visits; Points; Buy 1 Get 1 Free; Product (SKU) Specific; Department Group; Supplier Based; Tender Type; Specific Date & Time; Day of week/month; Customer Segments; Individual stores; Groups of Stores; 100's of Options

= Customer Insights =
Who buys what product?; How often?; How does promotion A compare to promotion B?; Which special should you run on
Tuesdays?; Which customers are about to leave and what can you do about it?; Know who has the best chance of responding
to a price promotion on product A; Which products retain customers and which ones don't?

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to your WooCommerce settings page and click on the 'Loyalty Box' tab
1. Sign up with Loyalty Box and input the details into form
1. Save your changes and your loyalty program will be applied to your WooCommerce store!

Yes.  You should visit the website at [Loyalty Box](http://www.loyaltybox.com "Loyalty Box") and sign up.
